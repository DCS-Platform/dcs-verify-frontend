1. What DCS Verify is

DCS Verify is a credential issuance and verification service built on the open R+2 standard. Issuers sign credentials as cryptographic receipts; anyone can verify those receipts — through our website, app, API, or entirely independently using the published standard. These Terms govern your use of verify.dcslabs.ai, api.dcslabs.ai, and related services (the "Service").

2. Verification is free and account-free

Verifying a credential requires no account and costs nothing. Verification results are cryptographic statements: that a signature verifies against a stated key, that content matches a hash, that a status bit is or is not set, and that an anchor exists on-chain. They are not legal advice, an endorsement of any issuer or subject, or a guarantee of any fact beyond the cryptographic checks performed. Advisory signals (portal risk, content analysis, fraud intelligence) are clearly labelled advisory and are never part of the cryptographic verdict.

5. Permanence — read this before issuing

Important: anchoring writes a hash (CID) of the credential into a public blockchain (Base). Hashes on-chain are permanent and cannot be deleted by anyone, including us. The hash itself contains no readable personal data. Receipts you give to holders are copies we cannot recall. Revocation marks a credential invalid in the public status list; it does not erase copies others hold.

6. Status and revocation

Issuers may revoke (permanent) or suspend (reversible) status-enabled credentials. Revocation cannot be undone — this is a deliberate property of the system. Status lists are public, anonymous bitstrings.
